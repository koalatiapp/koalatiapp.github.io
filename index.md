## Introduction

Koalati is a website testing platform that makes extensive use of open-source tools and librairies to provide powerful functionalities to web developers and agencies.

Whether you help maintain Koalati's existing tools and librairies or contribute fresh new tools of your own, everything you need to ensure your contributions are high-quality and production ready should be present in this documentation.

If you believe some information is missing from this documentation, please [submit an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/koalatiapp/koalatiapp.github.io/issues/new){: target="_blank"} or send us an email at [info@koalati.com](mailto:info@koalati.com).
